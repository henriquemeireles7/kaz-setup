## Your SaaS Template is Ready

Douala ships with everything a modern SaaS needs out of the box:

- **Authentication** — Email/password and OAuth via better-auth
- **Payments** — Stripe subscriptions with webhooks and customer portal
- **Database** — PostgreSQL with Drizzle ORM and type-safe queries
- **Email** — Transactional emails via Resend with branded templates
- **AI** — Anthropic Claude integration for AI-powered features
- **Analytics** — PostHog for product analytics (optional)
- **Storage** — S3/R2 for file uploads (optional)
- **Security** — CSRF protection, rate limiting, secure headers

## The Maximal Template Approach

Traditional templates start minimal and make you build up. Douala inverts this — everything is included, and you remove what you don't need. AI makes pruning near-free.

### How to Customize

1. Clone the template
2. Describe your product to an AI agent
3. Let AI remove the parts you don't need
4. Build your unique features on top
